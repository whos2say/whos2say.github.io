Who’s to Say ? What’s Possible.

Interest-driven programs that help young adults with intellectual and developmental disabilities build confidence, skills, and community.

Explore the Paths For Support Coordinators

The Possibility Paths

Each person’s journey is different. Our programs are designed to be mixed, matched, and adapted to individual interests and goals.

Digital Path

Digital Content Creator Pathways

Video editing, graphic design, photography, and production skills — built one project at a time, at each participant’s pace.

Best for: people who love screens, games, or creative apps.

Sports Path

Who’s to Say Sports

Challenger Sports, fitness activities, game outings, and simple sports media projects like highlights and commentary.

Best for: athletes, superfans, and storytellers.

Life Path

Life Skills & Independent Living

Routines, simple cooking, money basics, community navigation, and social communication — always personalized.

Best for: young adults preparing for greater independence.

Community Path

Community Integration & Advocacy

Supported outings and storytelling that help participants show up, speak up, and be seen.

Best for: self-advocates and community builders.

Maker Path

Maker Market & Events

Create, present, and celebrate art and small projects in supportive, low-pressure settings.

Best for: creators who love sharing what they make.

Entertainment Path

Entertainment Pathways

Acting basics, on-camera confidence, teleprompter reading, and voice work — turning passion into communication skills.

Best for: performers and personalities.

How Our Programs Work With DDD

Programs are funded through New Jersey DDD Goods & Services, allowing for flexible, interest-based supports.

Confirm DDD & Funding

Step 2

Select Possibility Paths

Step 3

Schedule & Begin

Start individualized or small-group sessions and evolve over time.
