About the Foundation

The Who's to Say ? Foundation is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit (33-3826585 ) based in Ocean County, NJ. We challenge low expectations by building spaces where creative young adults with intellectual and developmental disabilities can thrive—through community, practice, and purpose.

Inclusion

Everyone belongs. We design programs that honor unique interests, access needs, and communication styles.

Independence

We coach real-world skills—creative, social, and practical—so participants grow confidence inside and outside the studio.

Remix Mindset

We upcycle tools, stories, and identities. Participants remix culture to create original work and discover their voice.
