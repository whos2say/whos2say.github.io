About the Foundation

The Who's to Say ? Foundation is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit based in Ocean County, NJ. We challenge low expectations by building spaces where creative young adults with intellectual and developmental disabilities can thrive—through community, practice, and purpose.

Inclusion

Everyone belongs. We design programs that honor unique interests, access needs, and communication styles.

Independence

We coach real-world skills—creative, social, and practical—so participants grow confidence inside and outside the studio.

Remix Mindset

We upcycle tools, stories, and identities. Participants remix culture to create original work and discover their voice.

Programs

Our starter track supports skill‑building, career exploration, and daily independence. Available under NJ DDD Self‑Directed Goods & Services during our pilot.

Remix Studio — Digital Storytelling

Skill‑building • Social inclusion

Small‑group and 1:1 coaching in podcasting, video, streaming, and content creation using accessible tools (CapCut, OBS, Canva). Sessions center on personal interests like gaming, music, and sports, building communication and self‑advocacy through making.

Typical cadence: 2× per week • 2 hours • 3‑month pilot with monthly reviews.

Learn more

Supported Micro‑Enterprise

Entrepreneurship • Career development

A personalized creative business incubator. Explore and launch a simple micro‑enterprise (e.g., content channel, digital art, custom merch) using tools like Canva and Printify. Coaching covers branding, marketing, budgeting, and goal‑setting.

Typical cadence: 1× per week • ~3 hours • 3‑month pilot with review and extension options.

Technology for Independence

Assistive technology • Daily living

1:1 coaching to use everyday tech—calendar apps, reminders, transportation, visual schedules, and communication tools—to support time management, routines, and safer community navigation. Family/staff training available for consistency.

Typical cadence: 1× per week • ~90 minutes • Ongoing with quarterly updates.

The Remix Studio

A cozy, ocean‑inspired creative space with cameras, mics, and accessible editing stations—purpose‑built for learning by doing.

  • Beginner‑friendly workflows for podcasting, video stories, and streaming
  • Positive, strengths‑based coaching that meets participants where they are
  • Clear safety and consent guidelines for any public sharing
  • Pathways to showcase work, from private screenings to public channels
