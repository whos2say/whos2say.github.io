Purpose

Life Skills & Independent Living focuses on the everyday routines that build confidence, choice, and participation at home and in the community.

Core Life Skill Areas

Daily Routines

  • Calendars, alarms, and visual schedules

Home & Kitchen Skills

  • Simple meal prep and safety
  • Cleaning, organizing, and checklists

Money & Shopping

  • Handling cash or cards
  • Comparing prices and budgeting

Community Navigation

  • Using rides safely
  • Learning routes to familiar places

Social & Emotional Skills

  • Conversation and self-advocacy
  • Managing frustration and asking for help

Outcomes & Skills

  • More predictable routines
  • Increased home participation
  • Greater comfort in community spaces

Coordinator / DDD Summary

Individualized Goods & Services-funded supports focus on routines, basic meal prep, money skills, and community navigation to increase independence.
