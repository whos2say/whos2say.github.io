Purpose

Maker Market & Events is about creating and sharing. Participants design and make things—digital art, crafts, or small 3D prints—and practice presenting them in supportive, low-pressure environments.

How It Works

Creation Sessions

  • Digital or physical design sessions
  • Simple products such as stickers, prints, or small crafts
  • Building sets of items for display or online galleries

Presentation & Event Practice

  • Setting up tables or displays
  • Practicing how to talk about their work
  • Participating in low-pressure showcases or markets

Money & Value Concepts

  • Basic ideas of pricing and value
  • Understanding that creativity has worth
  • Exploring gifting or sales under guidance when appropriate

Outcomes & Skills

  • Pride in completed creative work
  • Improved communication and presentation skills
  • Early exposure to entrepreneurship concepts
  • Positive public experiences and recognition

Coordinator / DDD Summary

Goods & Services-funded sessions support designing and creating items and participating in small events. The focus is skill development, confidence, and self-esteem—not commercial pressure.
