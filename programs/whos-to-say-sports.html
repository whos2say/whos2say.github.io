Purpose

Who’s to Say Sports blends athletics, sports media, and community engagement. It’s for participants who love sports—whether they want to play, talk about the game, or help create coverage as part of a media crew.

How It Works

Using DDD Goods & Services, families can fund:

  • Transportation and support for Challenger or inclusive leagues
  • Outings to local school, college, or pro games
  • Sessions focused on filming, interviewing, and editing sports content

Program Elements

Sports Participation & Wellness

  • Joining Challenger or recreation teams
  • Practicing teamwork and sportsmanship
  • Accessible, enjoyable fitness activities

Sports Media & Commentary

  • Recording short recaps or fan takes
  • Interviewing teammates or coaches
  • Filming highlights and podcast segments

Community & Events

  • Group outings and watch parties
  • Spirit nights and team events
  • Opportunities to visit arenas or meet teams

Outcomes & Skills

  • Improved social skills and confidence
  • Stronger sense of belonging
  • Better communication through interviews and commentary

Coordinator / DDD Summary

Supports funded through DDD Goods & Services may include participation in inclusive sports, structured fitness activities, supervised outings, and sports media skill-building focused on communication and engagement.
