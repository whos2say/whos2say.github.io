Purpose

Entertainment Pathways is for participants who enjoy being in front of a camera or microphone—or want to explore it. The focus is on confidence, performance basics, and creative expression.

Core Components

On-Camera Performance

  • Reading from a teleprompter
  • Practicing short intros and segments
  • Simple acting scenes or role-play

Voice & Audio

  • Microphone use and voice recording
  • Voiceover scripts and character voices
  • Podcast-style conversations

Fandom & Media Literacy

  • Discussing favorite shows, movies, or creators
  • Learning about representation in media
  • Turning fandom into creative projects

Portfolio Building

  • Simple headshots
  • Short clips collected into a mini reel
  • Safe storage and sharing guidance for families

Outcomes & Skills

  • Increased comfort speaking and presenting
  • Better understanding of media and representation
  • Affirming experiences on camera or microphone
  • Creative materials for future opportunities

Coordinator / DDD Summary

Goods & Services-funded supports focus on communication, performance, and media literacy, resulting in improved confidence and a small collection of media clips or photos.
