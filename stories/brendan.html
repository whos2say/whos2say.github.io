The piece

He built his own Top 5 musicals, Broadway Mix.

Brendan has a keen ear for classic rock and a heart full of Broadway show tunes. So he made a playlist that’s entirely his — “DJ Brendan’s Broadway Mix” — and he navigates it with real independence, cueing the next number and practicing the introductions between every song.

For Brendan, music isn’t just entertainment. It’s a way to engage confidently in a dialogue about something he is passionate about.

The work

From fan to DJ: mastering the mix.

Step into Brendan’s world and you’ll find him creating his playlist with real independence — navigating Spotify, setting up collaborative shares, and practicing smooth transitions between his favorite Broadway anthems. He’s not just listening. He’s hosting and sharing, because he believes music is for everyone.

With personalized headphone coaching, he’s honing the craft of a radio show host one song intro at a time.

And the music is doing something bigger: his communication is flourishing — from simple responses to joyful, sustained, lyrical participation.

DJ Brendan Radio · on set

Behind the scenes

Powered by the Remix Studio.

Brendan’s growth is amplified by a professional, accessible space built for exactly this. The Remix Studio gives him real gear — a Blue Yeti microphone, a Rode Wireless GO II system, and intuitive audio software — plus a safe place to explore, experiment, and be heard.

It isn’t just about recording. It’s about building real-world digital media skills, at his pace, around his passion.

The pathway

Unique pathways, not generic training.

Who’s to Say builds possibility paths around an individual’s strengths — never a one-size-fits-all job program. For Brendan, that path sings. Here’s the Digital Content Creator pathway we’re building with him, and for every young adult who walks in next.

  1. 01 / Express

    Music as a voice.

    Broadway, classic rock, and a microphone. We follow what lights him up and let communication grow through the songs he loves.

    Entertainment pathway
  2. 02 / Create

    Real skills, real gear.

    Spotify navigation, playlist shares, mic technique, and clean transitions — on professional tools, with personalized coaching.

    Maker pathway
  3. 03 / Broadcast

    On air, and independent.

    DJ Brendan Radio — a show he hosts and publishes himself. The foundation’s vision of independent content creation, made real.

    Community pathway
